**Source:** CS2 35

A small camp close to the south mountains and just at the start of the salt flats.
House of a miraculous source of potable water in a vast desert of sand at west and sand at east. The camp is well hidden at the south of [[The Djurum]] desert and it's inhabitants rarely leave the place.

**Population:** 200 Human desert dwellers

## Important NPCs
- [[Murjana of the Hamad Tribe]] Human Lawful, Spiritual Leader
- [[Delila of the Hamad Tribe]] Human Neutral, Militar Leader
- [[Omid of the Hammad Tribe]] Human Lawful, Eldest son o Murjiana, Soldier servient to Delila.
- [[Remy the merchant]], Human Lawful Wealthy With a gold tooth that spits too much. Loves pet animals. Leads merchant caravans to civilisation to control goods trade. Works as quartermaster to [[Murjana of the Hamad Tribe]]
- [[Arlomas of the Silent Mountain]], Elf caotic Desert Rider, with long braided hair that prays aloud and is in a secret religious cult. Mounts and trains Scrag (CS2 pg 43) and also hunts them for food. Works as huntmaster to [[Delila of the Hamad Tribe]]
