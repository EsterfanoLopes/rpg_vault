_Manages supplies, repairs gear, and stretches resources. The backbone of any expedition._

The quartermaster helps the party on controlling the essential resources for the party, taking annointance of resources in the whole party in order to manage resources with the goal of making the expedition as successful as possible.

## Mule

_Will you finish that… or should we save this for later?_

- At morning, after the party consumes the ration necessary for traveling (1 per day), 1/3 (rounded down) of the consumed rations are saved with the quartermaster. This resources may be redistributed. They still consumes slots.
    

## Keeper

_You stretch a week’s rations to ten days_

- Once per expedition, produce 1 ration from "Hidden reserves".
    
- Rolls charisma test for Mounts with ADV.
    
- Gain 1 extra equipment slot.
    

## Fixer

_Rust and rot fear your upkeep_

- For up to equal to the INT MOD number of times, improvise the fix of a broken tool/gear/piece. Roll INT CD to be determined by the GM. On a success, the item can still be used with penalties. The item is totally fixed if you rolled a natural 20.
    
- Gains 2 extra equipment slots
    

## Hoardkeeper

_Your packs defy mortal limits_

- Once per expedition, declare that you packed the _perfect item_ (non magical, talk with GM for a reasoneable item). The value for the item must be repaied in order to recover the usage of this ability. Spend 1d100 gold pieces next time you are in a city to recover this ability.
    
- Party gains 1 extra slot (Quartermaster included).
