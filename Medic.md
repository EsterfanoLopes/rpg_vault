_Patches wounds, cures ailments, and keeps the party alive. Where others falter, they endure._

The medic may help heal injuries, poisoning, hard terrain conditions (extreme heat or cold) and other conditions.

**OPE** means Once per expedition. This means that this ability can only be used again after the medic is back to a safer place where it can refill it's materials. Also, **a medic must have it's medical tools**. The price to refill the materials is given in each ability.

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Level|Title|
|0|Cleaner|
|1|Bandager|
|2|Healer|
|3|Lifesaver|

## Cleaner

_You stop bleeding… mostly_

- **Care for all**: The help of a medic is reflected mostly in HP recovery. The cleaner may once per day, after the camp is up, to heal in 1d4 HP up to a number of characters up to MOD INT. Roll the dice once and apply the result of the die to every selected character.
    

## Bandager

_Poison and disease fear your craft_

- Once per day can try to neutralize a poison or disease. INT CD 15.
    
- The dice from _care for all_ increases to 1d6.
    

## Healer

_Your steady hand saves limbs_

- **Stabilize**: OPE* the medic may stabilize one creature without test. the cost to refill this materials is 3d4 GP.
    
- The dice from _care for all_ increases to 1d8.
    

## Lifesaver

_You wrestle souls back from the grave_

- **Save life:** OPE* The medic may revive with 1HP one of the fallen PCs that died in the last hour. The cost to refill this materials is 10d10 GP.
    
- The dice from _care for all_ increases to 1d10.
