_Boosts morale, inspires courage, and rallies the weary. A leader in word and spirit._

The motivator keeps the morale of the party high besides the challenges that the party may face. This includes spending time with their fellow party members and talking, entertaining, etc.

|   |   |
|---|---|
|Level|Title|
|0|Cheerful Mumbler|
|1|Rallying Voice|

## Cheerful Mumbler

_Your pep talks are… optimistic_

- The motivator can help up to a number of members of the party equals to it's CHA modifier per day.
    
- **Increase Morale**: PCs that the motivator is motivating receives +1 to every test related to the exploration rules.
    

## Rallying Voice

_Your words steel trembling hands_

- Once per day the party has advantage in morale checks faced in random encounters on HEX.
    
- Once per day, after the camp is build up, the motivator can recover 1 CON point from a PC that lost CON value due to effects of the expedition (as for example, fatigue for pushing pace).
    

## Soul Warmer

_People march into hell for your cause_

- Once per day one test related to exploration rules from another PC is rolled with ADV.
    

- _Increase Morale_ increases to +2.
    

## Heartfire Speaker

_Your presence turns routs to victory_

- Once per day, after the camp is build up, the motivator produces one luck token to up to CHA MOD members of the party.
